Privacy Policy for Muslimain

Effective Date: January 1, 2025

At Muslimain, your privacy is a priority. This policy outlines how we collect, use, and safeguard your personal information, especially as it pertains to your subscription and personalized learning experience. By using our app, designed to serve Muslims through features such as prayer times, educational content, and other services, you agree to this policy.

1. Information We Collect

We collect the following types of information to deliver a personalized experience and ensure the app functions properly:

a. Personal Information:

  • Email or Phone Number: For account creation and management.
  • Subscription Information: If you subscribe to our monthly or yearly plans, we collect information to process payments, though payment details are handled securely by third-party processors (e.g., App Store, Google Play).

b. Usage Information:

  • Personalized Learning: Data related to your learning progress and app interactions to offer tailored content and quizzes.
  • Location Data: Collected with your permission to provide accurate prayer times and masjid recommendations.

2. How We Use Your Information

We use your information solely for the following purposes:

  • Personalized Learning: To customize your educational experience within the app.
  • Location-Based Services: To provide accurate prayer times and masjid locations based on your geographic area.
  • Subscription Management: To handle monthly and yearly subscriptions, including payment processing.
  • Notifications: To send you reminders for prayer times and app updates based on your preferences.

3. Data Sharing and Disclosure

We do not sell, share, or disclose your data to any third party. The data collected is solely used for maintaining the app's core functionalities and your personalized learning experience. Your payment information is processed through secure third-party payment services, and we do not store this data.

4. Security

We take reasonable precautions to protect your personal data. Although no system can guarantee absolute security, we follow industry best practices to safeguard your information.

5. Your Control Over Data

You have control over the data you share with us:

  • Account Information: You can update or delete your email or phone number by managing your account settings.
  • Location Data: You may choose whether to enable location services for accurate prayer times and can disable it at any time through your device settings.
  • Subscription Management: You can manage or cancel your subscription via your app store account (Google Play, App Store).

To delete your account and all associated data, please visit our account deletion page.

6. Children's Privacy

Our app is intended for general audiences and does not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13 years of age. If we discover such information, we will take steps to delete it.

7. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. Any updates will be posted in the app, and the "Effective Date" will be revised accordingly.

8. Contact Us

If you have any questions or concerns regarding this privacy policy or your data, please contact us at admin@muslimain.com.

9. Data Retention

We retain your personal information only for as long as necessary to provide you with our services and fulfill the purposes outlined in this Privacy Policy. If you wish to cancel your account or request that we no longer use your information, please contact us at admin@muslimain.com.
